Healing Beats
Healing Beats is an interactive device designed to help the elders build up their hand muscles. This is my course work of junior's course "Product Design II" as individual work.
User Research
The main theme for "Product Design II" was "designing for the elders". As an important step of product designing, user research is necessary for us to know the real need of elder people and find the opportunity of designing. Our class went to a nursing home located in Xuhui, Shanghai to observe and interview the daily life of the elders.
Scenario and Persona
After researching in the nursing home, I with my two partners drew the scenarios and persona to summarize the result. In this way, we were able to conclude and determine the main problems in the elders' everyday life thus finding ways to design products for them.
Prototyping
I used Unity and Arduino as my software developing platform. The hardware device is an elastic ball bond with pressure detector built inside in order to get the pressing force from each finger respectively. The user need to squeeze the ball hard enough to perform actions in the Unity game. The Arduino device gets the numerical value of pressure and send it to the Unity. The force required to trigger an action is variable via Unity inspector controlling so I can adjust for different needs.
