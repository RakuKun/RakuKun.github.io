Healing Beats
Healing Beats is an interactive device designed to help the elders build up their hand muscles. This is my course work of junior's course "Product Design II".
User Research
The main theme for "Product Design II" was "designing for the elders". As an important step of product designing, user research is necessary for us to know the real need of elder people and find the opportunity of designing. Our class went to a nursing home located in Xuhui, Shanghai to observe and interview the daily life of the elders.
Scenario and Persona
After researching in the nursing home, I with my two partners drew the scenarios and persona to summarize the result.
