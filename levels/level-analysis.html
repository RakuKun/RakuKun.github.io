Level Design Analysis
From time to time, I write down my thoughts particularly on level design here. After all, one cannot gain without learning. (All pictures in the page are screenshots captured during my own gameplay.)
time spring 2020 - present
Uncharted 4: A Thief's End
One of the iconic action adventure games exclusively built for PlayStation, Uncharted 4 is a great example of using the whole set of game development tricks to weave levels that are strong in telling an impactful story.
Ghost of Tsushima
An open world action game, Ghost of Tsushima learnt from the genre and did a good job on making a vast, attracting oriental land that's strong in narrative and visual guiding.
Crysis 3 (WIP)
One of the most visually stunning games in 2013, the half-open-world Crysis 3 explores the level design further than its predecessor Crysis 2.
