Level Design Analysis
From time to time, I write down my thoughts particularly on level design here. After all, one cannot gain without learning. (All pictures in this page are the screenshots from my own gameplay.)
time spring 2020 - present
Uncharted 4: A Thief's End
One of the iconic action adventure games exclusively built for PlayStation, Uncharted 4 is a great example of using meshes, colors, and lights to weave levels that are strong in telling story. (WIP)
level design
Ghost of Tsushima
An open world action game, Ghost of Tsushima learnt from the experience of other genres and did a good job on making a vast, attracting oriental-style land without leaving players not knowing what to do.
Crysis 3
One of the most visual stunning games of that time, the half-open-world Crysis 3 explores the level design further than its predecessor Crysis 2. (WIP)
