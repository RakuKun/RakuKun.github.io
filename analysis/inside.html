INSIDE: Design and Visual
INSIDE is a puzzle-platformer adventure game developed and published by Playdead in 2016. The highly stylish graphics makes this small game feel emotional, and its design especially narrative part works well in telling a dystopian story without words.
time spring 2019
Design and Narrative
INSIDE goes beyond puzzle-platformers by telling a thrilling and open-ended story.
puzzle design
simple scene
Each scene in INSIDE is connected by puzzles, usually at most one puzzle for one scene. So the puzzle in form is not connected, and as the result each is not very complicated. INSIDE makes its puzzles feel "connected" with the continuity of the world and items - like the submarine in underwater levels.
puzzle types
Generally there are two prospective to start thinking of puzzles: making difficult one that offers unlimited time for thinking, and simpler one that requires correct input under time limitation. The two kinds of puzzles could balance the game difficulty and serve the narrative: when the character faces a puzzle where no one is here, it could be rather large scaled and complicated. If he is being chased by a hound, the puzzle is more like an adventure as time for reaction is short as he keeps running. After all, puzzle design serves the story. If it is annoyingly hard the story flow will be interrupted.
narrative
storytelling
Almost a direct contrast to text-rich game like Firewatch, INSIDE tells the story using only environment. The approach is suitable for players who likes the openness of the narrative, but not those who prefer a clear explanation - all except developers can only guess what the story is, but probably never 100% clear.
pace
INSIDE keeps its pace balanced by adding transition scenes between puzzles: the character would walk a little bit after solving a puzzle and before running into a new one. Like mentioned above, time constrained puzzles offers more nervosity while unlimited time puzzle is a good chance to slow down the pace and is a good save point.
Visual
The graphics in INSIDE is impressive - it keeps Limbo's dark ambience, but with the transition from black-white to fully colored world, the game has much stronger feeling of a dystopian world.
color
desaturated world
Everything in INSIDE has less saturated color than normal, and this feels like we are seeing the world through fog, which matches feeling of a dystopian world.
Warm colors generally give a feeling of hope and empowered. However, even in rare occasions in INSIDE where we can see warm lights, it is hard to feel the character's situation is getting better. The reasons is that first, the warm color is much less saturated than it should be naturally; second, the scene is blurred, indicating a unknown and fragile hope.
shape
Everything in INSIDE is not complicated in shape, what makes it feel good to watch is the good use of contrast and pattern. INSIDE is successful in using large shape volume and darker color to make the feeling of distance between the boy and the machine world.
contrast
INSIDE uses highly contrasted graphics to help add ambience to its story. One of the basic combination is darkness and light. Darkness means unknown and danger and makes major part in background and the path ahead. Light means certainty and hope so is shown for hints around the character. However, the neon light from a dark panel serves as one of the exceptions where light can also be evil.
Loneliness is also everywhere in the game. In the picture above the boy drives a submarine whose light is pointing at a naked figure with long hair (it). It is dangerous but afraid of light, so to beat the level player needs to control the boy and the direction of light so it is always lighting it. In this strange situation, the light connects the only two characters lone in the level, yet they keep a hostile relationship. Such scene really triggers the inner loneliness of me.
pattern
Pattern is the group of shapes. INSIDE uses the arrangement of repeated shapes to show the characteristic of a dystopian world.
These two pictures depict a place where human figures are eerily hanged inside some fluid. The composition is strange in nature as the fluid seems floating not affected by the gravity force. The pattern of human figure and machines there makes the scene even scarier, indicating the large scale of human experiment.
