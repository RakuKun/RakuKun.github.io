Plan: An Experiment
The Plan is an experience by Krillbite Studio. It is so short and special that I can hardly find a similar one to compare - it is more like a visual novel with a little interaction. On Steam its review has remained "Overwhelmingly Positive" since the release and most comments talk about their feeling and thoughts after playing. This game absolutely has some magic to attract so many players in only 5 minutes' length.
tempo summer 2019
Methodology
Before setting off the production, it is very important to have an overview of what the game ‘feels like’ and how we deliver side missions. It is a high fantasy world with many imaginary creatures and magic.
system overview
adventure system
Adventure System is a collection of gameplay, content and reward. It aims to encourage players to explore the game by providing nice single player focused experience. Most missions in Adventure System are not compulsory, about 5 minutes long, and challenging but also rewarding.
mount system
Mount System is a very special one by which character may transform into another species in the game, switching their models, animations, skills, even the camp. For instance, if the character becomes a crab, they walk sideways, use claws to attack, and become friends with other NPC crabs. The system grants designers brand new ways to think about how living things in the game interact with each other, and how to implement unforgetable experience using it.
photography system
Photography System is not a rare system in MMO games, but the one in Revelation has some cool features such as capturing ghost creatures and recognising NPC. This opens opportunity to implement it into missions.
audio system
There is a special item to play sounds in the game, and player collects sound under the framework of Audio System. It offers interaction with the sound, such as affecting some creatures, adding buff, and triggering events.
design principle
combine systems
My mentor and I both agree that the most fun comes from the cross-product of multiple systems. There are 4 special ones listed above, and of course many basic systems (economy, battle, mission, achievement) are so fundamental that we cannot go without them.
One of the practical ways is to think of the components of gameplay and try finding some possible options for each. The formula is:
Interaction Function * Entity * (Other) = Complete Gameplay
reward
It is better to reward players for exploring Adventure System as the missions are additional and optional. We decided to use a unique currency (called "Rainbow Jade") for missions so it won't affect in-game economic balance and is easier to determine the price of items.
Production
During the 2 month I analysed many existing gameplay and created many new ones.
ideation
easy story with ups and downs
It will be a 4min experience including interaction time so do not make the story too complicated. We go for a classroom romance since everyone probably has some experience on that. And we think adding a little surprise in story would help with the emotional flow.
meaningful interactions
We learn from What Remains of Edith Finch combining interactions with story. We leave important moments waiting to be accomplished by player interaction so they feel being in the situation rather than watching a story sequence.
