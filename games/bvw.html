Building Virtual Worlds
Building Virtual Worlds is one of the courses in my first semester in the Entertainment Technology Center, CMU. Interdisciplinary teams build rapidly prototyped immersive interactive virtual worlds. The course will cover world building, environmental design, non-linear storytelling, and related topics. My roles are usually game designer and 3D artist and sometimes I work on Unity programming and shaders.
Round 1
Round 1's theme is "help" and our team created a game "Sneaker" which is about directing a robot to get out of enemy zones. The hardware platform was MagicLeap AR. I took part in designing the game mechanics, creating player robot and doing shader effects.
Round 2
Round 2 was about creating a sense of "feeling free". Our game in Vive VR is about playing as a bird and flying to islands to find other birds who offer to colorfy our feather. My work includes game design, world layout, 3D modelling for the birds and animations.
Round 3
Round 3 is a lightning round of just one week for creating games that offer simply fun, replayable experience. Our game uses Jam-O-Drum hardware and made a game where we put four players on a collapsing island and to control firetrucks 2v2. I participated in game design, 3D art as well as programming for visual arts
Round 4
Round 4 a storytelling round. Our team built a game about school love using Oculus VR. During the experience players go through a short love story travelling between a dream world and reality. I created the game structure, designed game mechanics, made all human models and animated them.
