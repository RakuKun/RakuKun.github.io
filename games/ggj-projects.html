Global Game Jam Projects
During the GGJ of year 2018 and 2019, two crazy games were created by my friends and me. They are in no ways well polished but somewhat are representative of our wildest ideas.
Another Way Out
This is a horizontal action game based on the contradictions between young Chinese people and old customs. In this game the protagonist wants to escape home when he has to deal with his parents and relatives in Chinese new year. They become giant bosses in the game, even with a machine body. you can only depend on powers and skills learned from game memes to defeat them and escape home!
I worked as game designer and programmer during GGJ 2019 in Shanghai.

Dramatic Successor
Dramatic successor is a 2D action game where player plays as the successors of information in 3 eras. Different successors have different goals and abilities. It is designed for giving a brief understanding of the processing of media's development.
I worked as game designer and made some UI elements during GGJ 2018 in Shanghai.
