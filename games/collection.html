Game Collection
Game Collection is the showcase of some of the highly experimental or developing games. I like to make small prototypes to try some new mechanism, implement new art style or build some testing levels. Yet as most part of them are done by myself in a short time, they may appeal less polished.

Also during the Global Game Jam in 2018 and 2019, two crazy games were created by my friends and me. They are done within 48 hours and are the proud representatives of my team's wildest ideas.
time 2016 - 2020
work game design, art, programming
Dramatic successor was built during GGJ 2018. It is a 2D action game where player acts as the successor of information in 3 eras. Different successors have different goals and abilities. It is designed for giving a brief understanding of the history of media's development. Check our GGJ webpage.
time winter 2018
platform Unity
genre platformer
work game design, UI, programming
design elements
theme
The theme of GGJ 2018 is Transmit. We start thinking of the theme with several different approaches: transmit some information, some physical things (like waves), or some abstract concept in mankind civilization. Our final decision is to do a game about transmitting information throughout the history, so the first level is a soldier in Marathon carrying information for his country, the next scene is the travelling across the Great Wall, and the final scene is a modern scene on the topic of the internet and virus.
art style
We use comic style art for the game as it is fast to do in 2D and very expressive in platformers.
Another Way Out was built during GGJ 2019. It is a horizontal action RPG whose story is based on the contradictions between young Chinese people and old customs. In this game the protagonist wants to escape home where he has to deal with his parents and relatives in Chinese new year. They become giant bosses in the game, even with an eerie machine body. The player can only depend on powers and skills learned from game memes to defeat them and escape home! Check our GGJ webpage.
time winter 2019
platform Unity
genre action RPG
work game design, programming
design elements
theme
The theme of GGJ 2019 is Home. It is a very familiar topic, yet to be honest, few of us really have really thought about how to convey home by a novel gameplay. Actually our team had a hard time deciding what the game would be like, and finally we settled down making a action platformer whose story reflects the phenomenon nowadays in many cities in China: younger people don't want to hang out with their elder relatives because the youth think traditional etiquette is out of fashion and meaningless.
We imagine that this conflict will develop into worse situations where two generations don't want to talk to each other. So we kind of put our thoughts in a wilder level by this game.
art style
Black and white mostly is cool. With some color we are able to emphasize on the attacks so the players could recognize boss's action easily.
Chrono Bathhouse is a management simulation game where player run a bathhouse to serve some strange guests.
time summer 2019
platform Unity
work game design, programming and UI (partly)
design elements
ideation
The rule of Chrono Bathhouse is quite simple - to earn as much as you can in the restricted time. Slimes and Terracotta are the customers in this world and they appear and acts differently. I intended to add more diverse customers but that would take some more time.
art
The 2D Art below shows the art style of this game. I tried to draw NPCs in a cuter way - making them appealing to the players as much as I can. The work is mainly about drawing different washing animations for each type of customers in each facility. Thankfully two kinds of slimes only differ in color, so I only need to draw one group of animations then change color style.
Light Ones is an experimental game where a boy talks to a tree. It has a narrative system that influences the gameplay.
time winter 2019
platform Unity
work personal project
design elements
ideation
The story of Light Ones is really simple - you can almost say there is no traditional story inside. And there is almost no so-called interactions: all you need to do is keep the boy talking to the tree and listen to each dialogues. I want to keep this game as simple as possible so I can focus on the dialogues and background settings. There is nothing impossible in a dream world - but do characters in the game really dream? They are merely the collection of codes, images and 3D models. So when designing this game I keep thinking of the idea "meta-game", a concept that is not strange to hear nowadays. And I want to explore and bring the possibilities of "meta" element.
art style
All the art resources except skybox related are made on my own, of which I have spent most time working on is the character. He has no name so I would just call him "the boy". When shaping and rigging the boy I referred to the boy in game Inside. In order to make this game a bit more of fun, I designed animations for the boy so he can perform actions to show his feelings and emotions in the game. All the 3D and animation work is done in blender.
As for the art style of the whole game, I chose low poly with some ambient bloom so the work is easier but still has acceptable looking (at least I think so!).
Firefly is an action game exploring how to trigger player's emotion with game mechanism.
time winter 2018
platform Unity
work personal project
design elements
ideation
I got the conception of this game by thinking of a Chinese idiom "A flying moth darts into the fire" which sacrifice oneself for meaningless purposes. I wonder if there is a way to let the player act as a small thing but act more meaningfully? So came out Firefly.
interaction
The player controls a firefly that uses its light to guide a lost moth to find its way home. But the moth does not trust the firefly to some extent. According to the distance between the firefly, the moth acts in three different patterns:
· If the firefly is too close, the moth gets threatened and tries to escape to a "safer" distance.
· If the firefly is too far to see, the moth can easily lost its way and wander around aimlessly.
· The moth will only be willing to follow firefly while the firefly is neither too close or too far from it. Even in such case, the moth tries to keep a distance and follow the track subsequently.
The difficulties of this game is that, the firefly needs to keep an appropriate from the moth while ensuring both safety. There are challenges everywhere: environment, predators and limited light source.
experience
All games provide players with certain kind of experience. In firefly I want to do experiment with a dangerous adventure that awards nothing seemingly. The gameplay may offer some extent of incitation as player must avoid some danger, but in the end the failure is destined and no one will thank for guiding the moth. What will player feel and will they play this again? That is my question for the game.
time summer 2016
platform Arduino
work interaction design, programming
