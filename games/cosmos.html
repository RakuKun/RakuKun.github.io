COSMOS (ongoing)
COSMOS is a board game I am working on as the course project for Stone Librande's Game Design Fundamentals class. In general, this is a 1v1v1 deck building game with three different camps and unique cards for each.

Before thinking how we build games based on State Share, we agreed that it is very important to determine the advantage of Google Stadia and especially State Share that we are working on. After reading the posts on developer website we concluded some merits that differentiate State Share with traditional online or local multiplayer network.
1. State Share works well with Youtube streaming as it posts a link in the chat and audience can jump into the game simply by clicking the link. This enables new ways for streamers to interact with their audience.
2. State Share will record last 30s video before creating the link, and all information during that time will be included in the link. The developers have much freedom to decide which and how the information is to be used.
3. State Share will work with other Stadia features such as Crowd Play and Stadia Team is working to bring more flexibility to its function. In that sense, we as developers would concentrate on finding a well structured gameplay that manipulates State Share as its core mechanics.
