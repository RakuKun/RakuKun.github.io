Firefly
Firefly is an experimental game about guiding and sacrifaction. This is my individual work, the video will be posted very soon!
Emotion
I got the conception of this game by thinking of a Chinese idiom "A flying moth darts into the fire" which means doing useless, even harmful things. I wonder if there is a way to let the player act as a moth-like thing but act more meaningfully? I want to let the player feel a different emotional experience - more like the proudness of helping others.
