Light Ones
Light Ones is a visual fiction talking to itself on what is dream and reality. This is my individual work.

Download playable file
Storytelling
The story of Light Ones is really simple - you can almost say there is no traditional story inside. And there is almost no so-called interactions: all you need to do is keep the boy talking to the tree and listen to each's dialogues. I want to keep this game as simple as possible so I can focus on the dialogues and background settings. There is nothing impossible in a dream world - but do characters in the game really dream? They are merely the collection of codes, images and 3D models. So when designing this game I keep thinking of the idea "meta-game", a concept that is not strange to hear nowadays. And I want to explore and bring the possibilities of "meta" element.
Modeling
All the art resources expept skybox related are made on my own, of which I have spent most time working on is the character. He has no name so I would just call him "the boy". When shaping and rigging the boy I referred to the boy in game Inside. In order to make this game a bit more of fun, I designed animations for the boy so he can perform actions to show his feelings and emotions in the game. All the 3D and animation work is done in blender.
As for the art style of the whole game, I chose low poly with some ambient bloom so the work is easier but still has acceptable looking(at least I think so!).
